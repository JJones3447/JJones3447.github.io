About Me

Driven by growth, family, teamwork, and a desire to build useful software.

Early Life

I grew up in DeKalb, Illinois, where I spent most of my childhood. During my early adolescence, I was heavily into video games like Call of Duty, Minecraft, and Grand Theft Auto. When I was not gaming, I enjoyed going outside to play sports with friends.

As I got older, my interests shifted more toward sports, especially football, which I played from eighth grade through my sophomore year of high school. Eventually, I began focusing more seriously on academics and my long-term future.

Working With Me

I value communication, teamwork, and healthy relationships with coworkers. I believe strong team chemistry is important for producing quality work, no matter the size of the project.

While I may be quiet in a new environment at first, I adapt quickly once the ice is broken. I enjoy learning from others, contributing where I can, and working with people toward a shared goal.

Legacy & Inspiration

I am proud to be a first-generation college graduate. Being the oldest of six grandchildren motivates me to set a positive example for my family.

My father has been my greatest source of inspiration. His resilience, work ethic, and willingness to provide for others have shaped the kind of person I want to become.
