Education

Dekalb High School

I am a proud graduate of the Class of 2020 from DeKalb High School, where I spent all four years of my high school education. I earned a GPA of 3.943, placing me among the top 40 students in my graduating class.

Eastern Gateway Community College

After high school, I attended online courses at Eastern Gateway Community College from 2020 to 2022, where I earned an Associate of Arts degree with a 4.0 GPA. I completed the program with the intention of transferring to a university to continue my education.

Aurora University

When I transferred to Aurora University in the fall of 2022, I was still undecided about my major. I met with a counselor to explore different classes and fields that might interest me. He suggested I take a programming course to see if computer science might be a good fit, as it's a rapidly growing field. After completing that class, I knew that computer science was the path I wanted to pursue. In May 2025, I achieved a lifelong dream by becoming the first member of my family to graduate from college.
